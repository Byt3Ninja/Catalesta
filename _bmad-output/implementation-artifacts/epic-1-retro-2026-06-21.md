## 2. What went well (keep these habits)

1. **Disciplined deferral with a paper trail.** Every story recorded what was punted and why (modal semantics → first modal story; dark-mode toggle → operator settings; Action Center → later; clone/template UI → API+test level; FR-062 limit banner → P1b). No silent scope drift — confirmed as the habit to carry into Epic 2.
2. **Kernel reuse / convention-mirroring.** `ProgramVersion`↔`FormVersion`; cohorts-API↔programs-API↔orgs-API; `ConsentProvider`↔`DirectionProvider`. Low-risk by construction.
3. **Forward dependencies threaded cleanly.** 1-1's `// Story 1.5:` HomePage stub → fulfilled in 1-5; EntitlementService socket built in 1-4 → reused by 1-2's publish gate.
4. **Code review as a real gate.** Caught genuine issues every story; adjudicated candidates against source rather than rubber-stamping.
