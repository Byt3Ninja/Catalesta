# SaaS UX: Billing, Usage, Domains, and Branding

## Billing Workspace

Visible only to authorized tenant billing roles.

Screens:

- current plan
- usage dashboard
- plan comparison
- add-ons
- payment method
- invoices
- renewal
- upgrade
- downgrade
- cancellation
- billing contacts
- tax details

## Usage Display

Examples:

- Active programs: 4 of 5
- Annual applications: 1,850 of 2,500
- Staff seats: 12 of 15
- Storage: 72 GB of 100 GB
- Custom domains: 1 of 2

## Domain Setup Wizard

1. Choose platform subdomain or custom domain
2. Validate domain format
3. Display DNS instructions
4. Check verification
5. Provision TLS
6. Preview tenant portal
7. Activate domain
8. Display health status

## Branding Setup Wizard

1. Upload logo and favicon
2. Set portal name
3. Select approved colors
4. Configure footer and support contact
5. Preview desktop, mobile, Arabic, and English
6. Publish
